What the heck is Pledger?
Pledger is a new economy loan marketplace. We are digitally native for digitally natives. We provide non-dilutive financing for cool kids like you because we enjoy what you’re doing and we want you to grow!
What will you do if I default?
We believe that you won’t, we examine your accounts to make sure you are solvable. We also ask you to provide us with your account login and password. In case you cease loan repayments, we will have to withhold your account until the full amount plus interest is repaid.
Are you a bank? How do you make money?
No, we’re not a bank. We are a platform that connects lenders with borrowers. We charge lenders 1% on each loan they provide once the loan is fully repaid.
I’m a liquidity provider. Why should I invest in a loan with you?
With Pledger, you achieve a guaranteed yield at low risk and support the fast-growing creator economy at the same time. We rely on a proprietary risk-scoring model customized for creative content monetization metrics. Our borrowers are solvable and highly discouraged to put their social reputation at risk.
